About Our Mission

Nairobi East Seventh-day Adventist Church is a vibrant faith community dedicated to sharing the everlasting gospel. We strive to be a lighthouse in Nairobi, fostering spiritual growth and preparing a people for the glorious appearing of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Nairobi East Seventh-day Adventist Church exists to preach the everlasting Gospel of God’s love in the context of the Three Angels’ Message.
